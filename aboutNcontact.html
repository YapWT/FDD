About Us

Cotton Tufting Club was born from a shared love for the therapeutic and expressive art of tufting.
Founded in 2024, we are a community space for anyone passionate about creating unique and beautiful tufted rugs, wall hangings, and textile art.




Our Mission

  • Empower individuals of all skill levels to explore and enjoy the art of tufting.
  • Foster a supportive and inclusive community where members can learn, share, and inspire each other.
  • Promote the appreciation and awareness of tufting as a unique art form.





Our Values

  • Creativity: We believe that everyone has the potential to express themselves creatively through tufting.
  • Community: We are committed to fostering a welcoming and supportive environment for all members.
  • Learning: We encourage continuous learning and exploration within the art of tufting.
  • Quality: We strive to provide high-quality resources, workshops, and events for our members.



Our Team

Cotton Tufting Club is led by a team of passionate individuals with diverse backgrounds and a shared love for tufting.
We are dedicated to providing a positive and enriching experience for all members.



Join the Cotton Family

We invite you to become a part of our vibrant and supportive community.
Whether you're a seasoned tufter or just starting out, we welcome you to explore, learn, and create with us!
