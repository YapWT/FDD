Suitable for individuals interested in combining visual art with the tactile nature of tufting.

1 - 4 Persons

8AM - 5PM


RM70 (Per Pax)

Set Up Workspace (8:00 AM - 8:30 AM)

  • Prepare your tufting frame or machine.
  • Gather all necessary materials: yarns, tufting gun/needle, scissors, backing fabric, and design template.

Design Preparation (8:30 AM - 9:00 AM)

  • Review your design plan or pattern.
  • Transfer or sketch your design onto the backing fabric.

Tufting (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)

  • Start tufting according to your design.
  • Begin with outlining major shapes and filling in details.

Lunch Break (12:00 PM - 1:00 PM)

  • Take a break to recharge.

Continued Tufting (1:00 PM - 4:00 PM)

  • Resume tufting, focusing on completing different sections.
  • Manage your time to ensure even progress across the rug.

Final Touches (4:00 PM - 5:00 PM)

  • Review your work and make any necessary adjustments.
  • Trim excess yarns and tidy up edges.

Clean Up (5:00 PM - 5:15 PM)

  • Organize your workspace.
  • Clean tufting tools and equipment.

Adjust the timing based on your own pace and the complexity of your design. Happy tufting!
