FaQs

  • How do I book an outdoor activity with Campus Wilderness?

    • Booking your adventure is quick and easy! Simply navigate to our website, choose your preferred activity, select the date and time that are available, and follow the simple booking instructions.

  • Are the outdoor activities suitable for beginners?

    • Absolutely! Campus Wilderness caters to all skill levels. Our experienced guides ensure a safe and enjoyable experience, providing support and guidance for beginners while offering challenges for the more experienced.

  • What should I bring for the outdoor activities?

    • Each activity may have specific requirements, but generally, we recommend wearing comfortable outdoor clothing, sturdy footwear, and bringing essentials like water, sunscreen, and a hat. Check the specific activity details on our website for any additional recommendations.

  • How can I pay for my booked activity?

    • Campus Wilderness accepts online payments through secure platforms. Simply use your preferred payment method during the booking process.

  • Is transportation provided to the activity locations?

    • Transportation details vary depending on the activity. Some may include transportation, while others may require participants to arrange their own. Check the specific activity details on our website for transportation information.

  • Are the activities available year-round?

    • Yes, many activities are available year-round, but some may be seasonal. Check our website for the latest information on activity availability and schedule.

  • What happens in case of bad weather or cancellations?

    • Your safety is our top priority. In the event of bad weather or unforeseen circumstances, Campus Wilderness may reschedule the activity or offer refunds.

  • Do I need any prior experience for specific activities?

    • Most activities are designed for participants of all experience levels. However, some may have specific requirements. Check the activity details on our website for information about prerequisites and recommended skill levels.

  • How can I get in touch with Campus Wilderness for further inquiries?

    • For any additional questions or concerns, feel free to contact us through out social media or with the WhatsApp number provided. We're here to assist you!
