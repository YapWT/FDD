FaQs

Can I join the club if I've never tufted before?

Absolutely! We welcome all skill levels, and our club is a great place to learn.

Are materials provided, or do I need to bring my own?

Basic materials are provided, but feel free to bring any special yarn or fabric you'd like to use.

Is there a membership fee for joining the Tufting Club?

No, membership is free for all APU students.

Can I participate in more than one tufting activity?

Yes, you're welcome to explore all three activities offered by the club.

Are there any workshops or tutorials for beginners?

Yes, we regularly host beginner-friendly workshops to help you get started.

Can I bring a friend who's not a student at APU?

While the club primarily caters to APU students, guest are welcomed as well.

What types of yarn are recommended for tufting?

Any yarn suitable for your chosen fabric is acceptable; experiment to find your preferred materials.

Can I bring my own tufting equipment?

Absolutely, feel free to bring your own tools if you have them.

How often does the Tufting Club meet?

We meet twice a week, and the schedule is available on our website.

Are there opportunities to showcase finished tufting projects?

Yes, we organize exhibitions and events to display our members' creations.
