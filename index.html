Cotton Tufting Club - Welcome!

Step into a world where threads come alive and creativity knows no bounds. The Tufting Club is not just a club; it's a vibrant community where passion meets craftsmanship. Whether you're an experienced tufter or someone curious to explore the world of textile art, we invite you to join our tapestry of creativity.

At the Cotton Tufting Club, we celebrate the art of tufting through three distinct activities: Mirror Tufting, Rug Tufting, and Group Tufting. Each session is a canvas for self-expression, a journey into the tactile beauty of threads and the shared joy of creation.

Our club is more than just a gathering—it's a place where friendships are woven, skills are honed, and inspiration is found in every tufted masterpiece. Join us in unraveling the threads of imagination, where every tuft tells a story, and every member contributes to the rich tapestry of the Cotton Tufting Club at APU.

Discover the joy of tufting, connect with fellow enthusiasts, and let your creativity soar. Welcome to a world where art and community intertwine—welcome to the Cotton Tufting Club!
